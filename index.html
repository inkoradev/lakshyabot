Lakshya Study Discord Bot

Lakshya is a powerful and purpose-driven study bot built to support productivity and maintain discipline in a Discord study server. It equips both staff and students with essential tools such as moderation commands, support ticket management, and focused study features like exam countdowns, VC monitoring, and quick access to rules — all designed to help users stay on track toward their academic goals.
